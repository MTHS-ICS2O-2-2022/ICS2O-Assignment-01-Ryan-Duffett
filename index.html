Who was Guido Van Rassum? Why was he important?
Guido Van Rassum is a Dutch programmer who is known as the creator of the Python coding language. During December 1989, he was bored and wanted to start a coding project to keep him occupied during Christmas. Since his office was closed, he started work on the coding language Python. He finished his work 3 years later. He was born in the Netherlands in 1965, and is now 67 years old. He received a master's degree in mathematics and computer science from the University of Amsterdam in 1982, which helped him in his coding journey. Later on in life, he worked at Google from 2005 to 2012, where he spent half of his time developing Python. ----
